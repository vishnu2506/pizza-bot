Yo Yo Pizza is a fast casual, family owned & run pizza business located in Mumbai, Pune, and Bengaluru. We operate in a quick service environment, with a focus on customer service and quality ingredients. All of that while creating a community around our pizza.
We opened our first shop in the heart of Edmonton in January 2019 and we quickly became a regular stop for the noon lunch crowd, meeting up for pizza and drinks with friends in the evenings and gathering with family on Sundays.
